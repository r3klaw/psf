about us

Peter Sindani Foundation was formed and registered in 2018. It is a recognized non- governmental organization (NGO) in Kenya. Its a non-profit making, non political and non-partisan Foundation founded on the pricipals and values of humanity, skilled population, poverty alleviation, quality medicare excellent education, appreciation of human labour , dignity and value among Kenyans and other Africans in rural areas of Africa.

Our vision is ‘Empowerment for success’

Mobile clinic

Kenya, like the rest of the third world countries has poor health care system. Most of the public hospitals are poorly equipped and have a perennial shortage of drugs. The rural areas of the county have no access to health care. This mobile clinic initiative seeks to address this gap.

Peter Sindani

Peter Sindani Founder

Mr. Sindani is a career administrator, with long experience in university administration.He worked as a senior Administrator, JKUAT University from 2010 to 2017. He has also taught Business and finance at the University. He is currently serving as an elected Member of County Assembly (MCA), and chairperson to the committee of Justice and Legal affairs of Bungoma County, a devolved unit of the Government of the Republic of Kenya.
